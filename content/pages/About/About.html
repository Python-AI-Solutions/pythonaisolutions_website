Our Story

Python AI Solutions was founded by John Lee, a neuroscientist turned software engineer and data scientist.


While earning a PhD from Trinity College Dublin, John realized the limitations of creating software solutions for science in constrained proprietary ecosystems. He pivoted to using open source tools to aid in conducting better science. Among other key advantages, these tools improve the potential for collaboration, scalability, and reproducibility.


Relocating to Washington, D.C., John spent five years working  as a data scientist and software engineer at the National Institute of Mental Health at the NIH campus, initially using the R programming language but quickly falling in love with Python. His work required extensive use of data pipelines to enable distributed computing on a high-performance cluster, and throughout this time, he provided  consulting and training to scientists on using R and Python  for programming and writing data pipelines. He used and contributed to open source tools developed on site at the NIH as well as those from other groups working in the field of human neuroimaging.


John's return to Ireland during the Covid-19 pandemic coincided with his transition to industry. Continuing on his journey of using open source software for data analytic needs, he worked as a software engineer and open source consultant at Quansight, a data, science, and engineering firm that specializes in solving complex, data-related problems by leveraging the open source software at the foundation of innovation in AI and machine learning. While there, he worked with clients ranging from non-profits to startups to large enterprises.


He founded Python AI Solutions to help organizations make use of the latest developments in open source software and AI, continuing with the same narrative of helping them transform their data into valuable insights in a sustainable, transparent way.
